Defusal

  • High Concept: A simple game where the objective is to defuse the bomb before the time runs out.
  • Player Experience Goals: Intense and time-preassured.
  • Genre: Guessing/probability
  • Game play: The player must make guesses on the 4 digit defusal code and then cut the correct wire before the time runs out. Initial phase does not require the correct order while the final entry must be in order.
  • Prototype goal: Figure out game states and different data structures in Phaser/js
  • Features: Keyboard and mouse inputs
  • Setting/Premise: You are the hero who needs to defuse a ticking bomb.
  • Story: none.
  • Target Audience: Anyone.
  • Play time: ~5 minutes
  • Strategies: Guesswork is the main strategy.
